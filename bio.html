Larry Zhang Biography

Background and Education

Hi, my name is Larry Zhang from Long Island, New York. I attended Ward Melville Senior High School in 2011. I am now currently a senior finishing up a biochemistry major, and computer science minor at Tufts University.

Work Experience

I currently work as a research technician at Tufts Medical. In my nearly two years there, we have finished running two studies, on a correlation between maternal B-vitamin intake and colorectal carcinogenesis and on the differences between genetic and food induced obesity on colorectal carcinogenesis.

Theta Chi Fraternity

I was elected as the Spring 2015 Marshal of Theta Chi Fraternity at Tufts. It is the Marshal's job to guide the new pledge class through the pledge process and to become full brothers. I was also the risk manager for through out 2014, in charge of ensuring the safety of brothers and guests during events and to work with the police during events and check-ins. I am very passionate about brotherhood and lending an assisting hand to brotherhood needs and philanthrophy events.

Hobbies

My biggest passions are playing and watching sports. I love playing intermural basketball, ultimate frisbee, tennis, volleyball, football. I spend a lot of my time playing these sports with friends and socializing through them.

In terms of watching sports, I follow the NBA and NFL very closely, especially the Kansas City Chiefs and Boston Celtics.
