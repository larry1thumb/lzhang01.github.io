About Me

Hi, my name is Larry Zhang and I was born in Stony Brook, New York. I attended school at the Three Village Central School District K-12 and graduated from Ward Melville High School in 2011.

In High School, all I wanted was to go to college near Boston. I would ignore pleas from my parents to go on college tours in Pennsylvania or down south, and adamantly said that I was only going to apply for schools in Massachusetts.
And thank God that worked out. I applied to Tufts University ED and was accepted January 8th, 2011.
